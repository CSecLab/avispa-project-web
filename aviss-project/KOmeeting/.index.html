Site

The Institute for Software Engineering (Lehrstuhl für Softwaretechnik und Softwareproduktionsumgebungen) of the Department of Computer Science of the Albert-Ludwigs-Universität Freiburg is in the ground floor of building 52 (Gebäude 52) in Georges-Köhler-Allee 52.

Here is a map of the campus, which you can reach by bus, by Straßenbahn, by car or foot. (If you get lost or have problems, phone Luca Viganò (0761) 203-8243 or 0179 1270109, or Ina Eckert (0761) 203-8240.)

BY BUS: (click here for a map of the busses and trams)

  • In the city center, e.g. at the stop Bertoldsbrunnen, catch bus #10, direction Paduaallee, and get off at the stop Bärenweg.
    Go back a few meters, take the first street left, go straight and then under the railway line - you are on the campus now!
    Go to building 52 (Gebäude 52), and in the ground floor look for the Institute for Software Engineering (Lehrstuhl für Softwaretechnik und Softwareproduktionsumgebungen).

    There is one bus approximately every 15 minutes (10 minutes in the rush hours), and the ride takes about 12 minutes.

  • Alternatively, in the city center you can also catch bus #11, direction Neue Messe / Ikea, and get off at the stop Fakultät für Angewandte Wissenschaften, which is directly in front of the main entrance of the campus.

    Tickets can be bought directly from the busdriver, but also at some newstands or in the RVF shop at the beginning of Salzstraße (near Bertoldsbrunnen).
    A ticket for a single one-way trip costs DM 3.50; `point'-tickets or daily tickets for 2 adults are also available.

To go back into town:

  • Walk back to the Bärenweg stop to catch bus #10, direction Merzhausen - Au.
    (Of course, this time you must catch the bus on the side of the road opposite to the one where you got off.)

    There is one bus approximately every 15 minutes (10 minutes in the rush hours), and the ride takes about 12 minutes.

  • Walk back to the main entrance of the campus and catch bus #11, direction .
    (Of course, this time you must catch the bus on the side of the road opposite to the one where you got off.)

BY STRAßENBAHN: at the Hauptbahnhof ( main train station) catch the train S0 (direction Breisach) and get out at the stop Neue Messe/Universität - you are on the campus now!

BY CAR OR FOOT: here is a map!

  • At the northern end of the main train station (Hauptbahnhof) turn left into Zur Unterführung (under the railway bridge), and go straight for approximately 2 kms.

    The name of the street you're on is Breisacher Straße, and almost at the end of it (after approx. 1.8 kms), on the right, you can also find the Hotel Boardinghaus, where some of the participants are staying.

  • At the crossing Breisacher Straße - Berliner Allee, turn right into Berliner Allee, and go straight for approximately 500 metres.

  • Go under the railway bridge and then immediately turn left into Georges-Köhler-Allee to enter the minicampus.
