Hi all,

just a small message to advertize the issue of an update for the xemacs 
mode.
It will be available in the next avispa tool release, but you can 
download it now from:
http://www.irit.fr/recherches/LILAC/Pers/Chevalier/

among other things..
key sequence+space        inserts
":a"              ": agent,"
":ch"              ": channel (dy),"
":f"              ": function,"
":m"              ": message,"
":n"              ": nat,"
":pi"              ": protocol_id,"
":pk"              ": public_key," nil 2
":sk"              ": symmetric_key,"
":t"              ": text,"
plus:
* ":enc" prompts to insert an encrypted message
plus
* no pretty-printing yet, but some auto-indentation stuff, insert of 
patterns
(e.g. b_r and c_r for respectively basic and composition roles)

Any feedback, especially before the tool release, welcomed !
Cheers,
Yannick Chevalier
