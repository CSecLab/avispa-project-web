Meeting location / how to get there

The meeting is located at this address:

Aula di rappresentanza (Representatives' room), I floor
DIST - Villa Bonino
viale Francesco Causa, 13
16145 Genoa (Italy)

An overhead projector, beamer and laptop will be available.

To get to the meeting location, choose either destination:

  • the neighbourhood of Albaro (via Albaro, junction with viale Francesco Causa). To get there by bus, take either nr. 15 or 41 and get down at the very junction;
  • the Casa Dello Studente (Student Accommodation Building) along corso Aldo Gastaldi. To get there by bus, take either nr. 17, 43, 44, 45, 85, 86 or 87 and get down at the Casa Dello Studente.
Once you have got to either place, use this map to get to the meeting location. On the map, number 1 indicates the meeting venue, number 2 indicates the Casa Dello Studente and number 3 indicates the junction of via Albaro and viale Francesco Causa.
