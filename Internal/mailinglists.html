Mailing lists

If you wish to send a message to the AVISPA community, you can choose one of the following email addresses.

  1. avispa-general is for general-purpose mail to all members of the project. Current members: all.
  2. avispa-tech is strictly for technical mail, i.e., implementation or theoretical issues regarding out theory and tools. Current members: all.
  3. avispa-admin is for administration and business matters. Current members: Alessandro, DavidB, LucaC, LucaV, Michael, Laurent, DvO, Jorge.
  4. avispa-modeling goes to the Modeling taskforce. Current members: paulh@inf.ethz.ch, compa@dist.unige.it, jacopo@dist.unige.it, David.von.Oheimb@siemens.com, boichut@lifc.univ-fcomte.fr, Judson.Santiago@loria.fr, Jorge.Cuellar@siemens.com, rlupu@elcom.pub.ro, moedersheim@inf.ethz.ch, lan_liu@web.de, sbracher@gmail.com, Monika.Maidl@siemens.com
  5. avispa-compiler goes to the HLPSL2IF/IF taskforce. Current members: paulh@inf.ethz.ch, moedersheim@inf.ethz.ch, Laurent.Vigneron@loria.fr, chevalie@loria.fr, jacopo@dist.unige.it, compa@dist.unige.it .
